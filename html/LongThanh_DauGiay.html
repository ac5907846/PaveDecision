FLEXIBLE PAVEMENT DESIGN:

  • Road Grade: Expressway (i.e., Freeway)
  • Total Project Budget: $638M (9,890.6 Billion VND in 2007, 1 USD = 15,500 VND)
  • Project Delivery Method: National budget capital and foreign loans
  • Locations: Dong Nai, Ho Chi Minh (South Region)
  • Length: 54.9 km (Design Report Chapter 2)
  • Lane: 4 lanes
  • Average width of each lane: 3.75 m/lane
  • Design life cycle time: 15 years
  • One day traffic loads: Ntt = 1,930 axles/day-night (calculated based on Design Report Chapter 6)
  • Average vehicle growth: 7.67 %/year (calculated based on Design Report Chapter 6)
  • Accumulated loads during the design time: Ne = 2,318,783 axles (calculated based on Design Report Chapter 6)
  • Standard load: 120 kN
  • Construction: 2009-2015
  • Discount Rate: 6.72%/year (Average from 2010 to 2023) (Design Report Chapter 21)
